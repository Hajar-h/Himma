The West

The western region has always been known for its natural artifacts, especially Jeddah

And it is currently evolving these treasures into technological and economic innovations, driven by the Vision 2030. These improvements aim to integrate technology with nature, creating a new balance that enhances both the environment and the economy.

Jeddah Central Project:

The project seeks to maintain Jeddah's rich heritage while integrating modern elements, showcasing the city's Hijazi essence in a contemporary way. It will implement top-tier sustainability standards and leverage data analytics for urban planning, allowing for informed decisions based on demographic and usage information.

Located in central Jeddah, it will include:

  • Integration of technology into urban management through smart solutions, enhancing services such as traffic management, waste management, and public safety.
  • Interactive Experience Zones: Development of smart recreational spaces that integrate augmented reality and digital interactions to enhance visitor experiences.

NEOM: A new Future

A futuristic city that is currently under development near the western region along the Red Sea, designed to operate entirely on renewable energy and will leverage cutting-edge technologies, including artificial intelligence and robotics, to create a smart, efficient, and sustainable living environment.

With a focus on innovation, the city aims to enhance the quality of life for its residents while minimizing its ecological footprint.

NEOM aims to create a fully integrated smart city that will feature:

  • Autonomous transport systems
  • Digital health services.
  • VR and AR technologies for entertainment, education, and tourism, creating immersive experiences for residents and visitors.
