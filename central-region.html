Qiddiya: The Ultimate Entertainment & Gaming Destination

Imagine a place as powerful as an airplane, involving multiple industries and redefining entertainment and technology that’s Qiddiya.

Qiddiya requires a completely different vocabulary due to its vast scope. One of its key goals is balancing Saudi identity with global reach and integrating Saudi Arabia’s natural landscape.

Located just outside Riyadh, Qiddiya will include:

  • Esports & VR/AR gaming center.
  • AI driven theme park experiences.
  • Smart traffic & crowd control with IoT.

The Mukaab: A Futuristic Smart City

Welcome to The Mukaab, a 400 meter high, fully immersive digital city in Riyadh’s New Murabba district.

This massive cubic skyscraper will offer virtual reality enhanced experiences inside a real world space, blending AI, cloud technology, and smart city innovations.

Technologies included:

  • AI powered automation.
  • VR/AR fantasy world experiences.
  • Cloud driven digital twin technology.

IBM’s Software Lab: Future of AI & Cloud Innovation

Riyadh is becoming a tech powerhouse, and IBM is leading the way with a $200 million Software Lab.

This Lab will include:

  • AI powered software solutions.
  • Advancing cloud computing.
  • Creating high-skilled tech jobs.

King Salman Park: Riyadh’s Smart Oasis

Riyadh is redefining urban spaces with King Salman Park, the largest city park in the world!

Spanning 13.4 square kilometers, this high-tech green escape blends nature, innovation, and entertainment.

Features:

  • AI powered irrigation & lighting.
  • Interactive AR navigation app.
  • VR enhanced museums & AI shows.
