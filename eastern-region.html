KING SALMAN ENERGY PARK (SPARK): A World-Class Ecosystem in the Heart of the Energy Market

King Salman Energy Park (SPARK) isn’t just an industrial city – it’s the future of energy innovation. Strategically located in Saudi Arabia’s Eastern Province, SPARK is where cutting-edge technology, smart infrastructure, and sustainability come together to power the energy sector.

SPARK is a key pillar of Vision 2030 by shaping the future of energy, innovation, and investment.

  • AI automates operations and improves efficiency.
  • IoT connects systems for smarter management.
  • Blockchain secures transactions and data.
  • Digital twins simulate processes to save costs.

THE RIG: The World's First Offshore Adventure Destination

This first-of-its-kind offshore tourism destination in the Arabian Gulf blends Saudi Arabia’s oil and gas heritage with cutting-edge adventure and entertainment. Spanning 300,000 sqm, it offers a dynamic mix of extreme sports, underwater exploration, and high-tech amusement experiences, all set in a unique industrial-inspired environment.

Expected to attract 900,000 visitors annually, THE RIG will drive tourism growth, create jobs, and position Saudi Arabia as a premier global adventure hub.

  • AI-Powered Safety Systems
  • Smart Booking & Hospitality Tech
  • Immersive Digital Experiences

AlKhafji Desalination Plant: The Future of Clean Water & Solar Power

TCombining clean water with clean energy, the world’s largest solar-powered desalination plant converts 90,000 m³ of seawater into fresh water daily using Solar SWRO technology. As a Vision 2030 powerhouse, it secures resources, lowers costs, and fuels local innovation.

As a Vision 2030 powerhouse, it’s not just making water it’s making history by securing resources, lowering costs, and fueling local innovation all by integrating:

  • AI for optimization
  • IoT sensors for monitoring.
  • Blockchain for transparent distribution.
  • Digital twins for predictive maintenance.
  • Cloud automation for improved efficiency.

Darin and Tarout Island: A Smart Future for a Timeless Destination

Bringing 5,000 years of history into the digital age, the Darin & Tarout Island Development Project is transforming this cultural gem into a high-tech, eco-friendly destination. With a SAR 2.64 billion investment, the project will integrate smart city solutions, AI-powered tourism experiences, and digital infrastructure to enhance quality of life and economic growth.

By 2030, it is expected to boost GDP by SAR 297 million annually, attract 1.36 million tourists, and create thousands of jobs – all while preserving the island’s rich heritage and natural beauty.
