Southern Region

The South

The goal is to develop the southern region, especially Asir region, to become a leading tourist destination on the global level.

Saudi Vision 2030 aims to develop the Southern Region, particularly the Asir region, to become a leading tourist destination at the global level. This is achieved by enhancing the tourism and entertainment infrastructure, which contributes to attracting local and international visitors."

Al-Soudah Development Project

Al-Soudah is Saudi Arabia’s highest peak, known for its cool climate and breathtaking landscapes. This project aims to transform the area into a luxury tourism hotspot, offering unique outdoor activities such as hiking, paragliding, and cultural experiences.

Features:

  • Expands the hospitality and tourism sector, attracting global visitors.
  • Promotes eco-tourism and sustainable development.
  • Enhances regional economic growth through investments in infrastructure.

Asir Development Strategy (Qimam and Shem)

The Asir Development Strategy, known as "Qimam and Shem", aims to boost tourism and economic development by investing in the region’s unique landscapes, infrastructure, and cultural heritage. The project will develop new resorts, enhance public services, and create entertainment hubs to attract over 10 million visitors annually by 2030.

Features:

  • Sports the diversification of the economy by reducing reliance on oil revenues.
  • Enhances quality of life through improved infrastructure and entertainment.
  • Promotes Saudi Arabia as a global tourism destination.

Jazan Special Economic Zone

The Jazan Special Economic Zone focuses on industrial growth, logistics, and investment. Due to its strategic location on the Red Sea, the project supports trade, manufacturing, and energy sectors. The zone offers tax incentives and business-friendly policies to attract global and local investors.

Features:

  • Strengthens Saudi Arabia’s global trade position by expanding industrial exports.
  • Supports economic diversification by promoting non-oil industries.
  • Encourages foreign direct investment (FDI) through business-friendly policies.

Najran Downtown Development Project

The Najran Downtown Development Project aims to transform the heart of Najran into a vibrant urban center that reflects the region's rich heritage and modern aspirations. The project includes the development of commercial spaces, cultural venues, and public parks to create a dynamic environment for residents and visitors.

Alignment with Vision 2030:

  • Economic Diversification: Boosts local businesses and attracts investments.
  • Quality of Life: Enhances urban living standards with improved infrastructure.
  • Cultural Preservation: Showcases Najran's unique history and traditions.
